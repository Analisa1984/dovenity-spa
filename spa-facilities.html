Spa Facilities

At Dovenity Spa, you will be greeted by a large range filled with luxury, serenity and tranquility. Whether you wish to explore our serene, delicate outdoor to begin nourishing your mind and body or head directly indoors to our nurturing ambient interior, you can choose as you need. Many clients first step out and take walks or picnics before venturing inside. We are pleased to inform you that our Spa is 900 square meters. We have to your disposal an outdoor pool, 2 indoor pools, an indoor thermal hot tub, 2 steam rooms, several massage rooms, 6 sauna rooms and 5 relaxation rooms. We are eager to get you started on your relaxation and recovery journey, feel free to book now.
